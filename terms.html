2. Description of the Application

SKN LABS is a mobile application for iOS that allows users to:

  • Scan cosmetic and skincare product barcodes and QR codes to retrieve ingredient lists
  • Analyse ingredient lists against user-defined allergy and sensitivity profiles
  • View informational safety assessments, scores, and badges based on ingredient data
  • Request community analysis of products not yet in our database
  • Build and manage a personal skincare routine
  • Access a history of previously scanned products

All ingredient data, safety assessments, scores, and badges provided by the Application are for general informational purposes only. They do not constitute medical, clinical, dermatological, or professional advice of any kind.
