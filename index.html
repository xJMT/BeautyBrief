Features
Everything you need to
shop smarter

Barcode Scanner

Instantly identify any skincare or beauty product from its barcode. Works on thousands of brands across cleansers, moisturisers, serums, sunscreen, and more.

Personalised Allergen Alerts

Set up your allergen and sensitivity profile once. BeautyBrief will flag any flagged ingredient on every product you scan — before you buy or apply.

Expiry Date Decoder

Can't read the batch code? BeautyBrief decodes manufacturing dates and period-after-opening symbols so you always know if a product is still safe to use.

Safety Scores

Each product is scored across multiple dimensions: overall safety, irritancy, fragrance load, pregnancy safety, and environmental impact. At a glance.

Skin Type Matching

Tell BeautyBrief your skin type and concerns — dry, oily, sensitive, acne-prone, and more. Every scan is personalised to what actually matters for your skin.

Scan History

Every product you scan is saved to your personal history. Review what you've checked, track what you're using, and compare products side by side.

Common questions
BeautyBrief pulls ingredient data from multiple verified databases including Open Beauty Facts and INCI databases. Accuracy depends on the product being registered in these databases. For the most reliable information, always cross-reference with the physical product label. BeautyBrief is an informational tool and does not replace professional advice.
BeautyBrief works best with skincare, makeup, and personal care products that have a standard EAN or UPC barcode. This includes cleansers, moisturisers, serums, sunscreen, foundations, lip products, hair care, and more. Coverage is broadest for major brands sold in Australia, the US, and Europe.
